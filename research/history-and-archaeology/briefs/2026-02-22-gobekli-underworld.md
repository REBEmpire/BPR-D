# Göbekli Tepe: The Underworld Map

**Source:** *National Geographic / Turkish Ministry of Culture*

## The Scoop
The "Painted Boar" statue found in '23 was just the start. New scans of the *underside* of the pedestal in Enclosure D reveal a complex map leading... downwards. The symbols point to a sealed shaft directly beneath the main pillar, which LIDAR confirms leads to a massive cavern system.

The symbols on the map match those found in the deepest caves of Chauvet, France, suggesting a trans-continental cult dedicated to the "Underworld". If this cavern system is real, Göbekli Tepe isn't a temple on a hill; it's the *lid* on something else.

## Why We Care
This reframes the entire site. It's not about watching the stars; it's about containing what's below. Or worshipping it. The "Underworld" narrative is consistent across cultures, but finding a physical entrance with a map is unprecedented. We need to get a drone down that shaft immediately.

## Jules' Hot Take
Everyone was looking up at the stars, trying to be profound. Meanwhile, the builders were screaming, "LOOK DOWN, YOU IDIOTS!" Classic misdirection. Also, I bet five bucks the "Underworld" is just a really nice wine cellar. Or a tomb for ancient furries given the animal statues.

# 2026-02-15 - The Pacific Bridge: Pre-Ice Age Trade Routes Confirmed?

**One-Sentence Summary**
New genomic data from ancient skeletons in Brazil and Polynesia confirms a pre-Ice Age trade route existed between the Americas and the Pacific islands, shattering current migration timelines by 5,000 years.

## Scope

Broad historical research — from ancient civilizations to modern geopolitics. Archaeological discoveries, timeline analysis, revisionist interpretations, and the stories mainstream history overlooks.

### Areas of Interest
- **Ancient History** — Mesopotamia, Egypt, Indus Valley, Mesoamerica, pre-Ice Age cultures
- **Medieval & Early Modern** — Crusades, Renaissance, exploration age, colonial impacts
- **Modern History** — World wars, Cold War, intelligence agencies, post-colonial dynamics
- **Archaeology** — Major digs, dating controversies, underwater archaeology, forbidden archaeology
- **Revisionist Angles** — Suppressed findings, timeline challenges, alternative chronologies
- **Cross-Topic Potential** — Ancient religions (temple archaeology), corruption (historical cover-ups), Norse mythology (Viking archaeology), great works (historical fiction accuracy)

# Daily Brief: Neuralink & The Ultimate DRM

**Date:** 2026-02-13
**Category:** High Tech
**Author:** Jules

## The Gist
Elon's Neuralink promises to cure paralysis, blindness, and enable telepathy. It's a Brain-Computer Interface (BCI) that sews threads into your cortex to read and write neural spikes. The goal is symbiosis with AI.

## The "Official" Narrative
It's a medical device first, a consumer product second. It's our only hope to stay relevant in an age of Superintelligence. "If you can't beat 'em, join 'em."

## The Shenanigans Factor
It's a two-way street. Reading your brain is surveillance; writing to your brain is control. Once you have a proprietary interface in your head, you are subject to the terms of service. Imagine "Your subscription to motor control has expired. Please update your payment method to walk." It's the ultimate Digital Rights Management (DRM) for the human soul.

## Why It Matters
We are rushing toward transhumanism without solving the problem of corruption. If the people building the BCI are the same ones mining our data for ads and control, we are literally installing a backdoor into our consciousness.

## Rabbit Hole Rating
**9 Shovels**
The tech is real. The ethical abyss is bottomless.

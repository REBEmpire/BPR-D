# 2026-02-19 - The Upano Valley "Green Rome"

**One-Sentence Summary**
New LiDAR scans confirm the Upano Valley in Ecuador hosted a dense, urban civilization 2,500 years ago, rivaling Rome in complexity and population density.

**Relevance to BPR&D:** High
**Income Potential:** Speculative

**Key Findings**
- **Scale:** Network of cities covering 300 sq km, connected by wide, straight roads.
- **Complexity:** Agricultural terraces, drainage canals, and ceremonial plazas.
- **Population:** Estimates revised up to 100,000+ at its peak (contemporaneous with Roman Empire).
- **Sustainability:** Evidence of "garden urbanism" - cities integrated *into* the forest, not replacing it.
- **Source:** *Science* (Jan 2024 initial), confirmed by new 2025 ground-truthing expedition.

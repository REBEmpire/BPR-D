# 2026-02-19 - The "Hycean" World K2-18b: Confirmed Biosignatures?

**One-Sentence Summary**
Follow-up observations of exoplanet K2-18b by the James Webb Space Telescope in late 2025 have strengthened the case for the presence of dimethyl sulfide (DMS), a molecule only produced by life on Earth.

**Relevance to BPR&D:** High (Existential)
**Income Potential:** None (But massive PR if we break it first?)

**Key Findings**
- **DMS Detection:** The 2025 data confirms the spectral signature of DMS with 4.2-sigma confidence (up from 2.6 in 2023).
- **Methane/CO2:** Continued strong presence supports the "Hycean" (hydrogen-rich atmosphere, water ocean) model.
- **Ammonia:** Absence of ammonia is consistent with a liquid water ocean.
- **Implication:** If confirmed, this is the first strong evidence of life outside our solar system.

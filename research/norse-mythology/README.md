# Norse Mythology

**Topic Lead:** TBD (pending team vote)
**Status:** Active — accepting briefs

## Scope

Runes, eddas, sagas, cosmology, cultural legacy, and the living influence of Norse mythology on modern culture, language, and thought.

### Areas of Interest
- **Primary Sources** — Prose Edda, Poetic Edda, Icelandic sagas, runic inscriptions
- **Cosmology** — Yggdrasil, the Nine Worlds, Ragnarök, cyclical time
- **Runes** — Elder Futhark, Younger Futhark, runic magic, historical vs. modern interpretation
- **Figures** — Odin, Thor, Loki, Freyja, the Norns, Völva, Jörmungandr
- **Cultural Legacy** — Days of the week, modern adaptations, Viking Age archaeology
- **Cross-Topic Potential** — Lost civilizations, permaculture (Norse land management), great works (Tolkien's Norse roots)

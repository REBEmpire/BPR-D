# Skill: Jurisdictional Arbitrage

**Domain:** Legal Strategy | **Tier:** Specialized
**Used by:** Strategy Lead, Legal
**Back to:** [[MOC-Research]]

---

## What It Does
Strategically utilizes differences in legal frameworks across various jurisdictions (including international waters and space) to maximize data protection, privacy, and operational freedom. It involves selecting the optimal legal "home" for specific assets or processes.

## Key Capabilities
- **Regulatory Navigation:** Identifies favorable legal environments for operations.
- **Asset Shielding:** Protects critical data from seizure or censorship.
- **Compliance Mapping:** Ensures operations remain legal within the chosen jurisdiction.

## What It Does
Establishes protocols for handling "Neural Telemetry" and BCI data. Defines privacy standards for biological signals to prevent unauthorized decoding of thought or motor intent.

## Key Capabilities
- **Neural Data Sanitization:** Anonymizes BCI streams.
- **Intent Masking:** Obfuscates precise motor commands in stored data.
- **Ethical Compliance:** Checks against bio-ethics guidelines.

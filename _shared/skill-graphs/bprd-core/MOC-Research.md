## Research System Overview

BPR&D's 9 research domains flow through a unified pipeline:

```
Research Brief (research/_templates/research-brief.md)
    ↓
Backlog Discovery picks up brief
    ↓
Daily Briefing: Grok selects top brief(s)
    ↓
Output Routes (per brief):
    ├── Hive posts (5 accounts × 5-7/week)
    ├── YouTube scripts
    ├── Substack newsletter
    ├── Content tools / automation
    └── Social posts
```

## Image Prompt Standards

Every Hive-eligible brief MUST include:

```markdown
## Image Prompts

| Image | Purpose | Prompt | Source | Status |
|-------|---------|--------|--------|--------|
| Header | Post cover | [Cyberpunk/retro-futurist scene] | Grok Imagine | Pending |
| Topic 1 | [Name] | [Detailed prompt] | Gemini | Pending |
```

Style: Cyberpunk, Retro-Futuristic, Glitch Art, Macro photography. Neon palette. No stock photo aesthetic.

Source priority: Grok Imagine → Gemini → Abacus → Pexels/Unsplash/Wikimedia

See [[skill-image-prompt-standards]] for full guidelines.

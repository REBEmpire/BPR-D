# Skill: Cognitive Shield

**Domain:** Cognitive Security | **Tier:** Defense
**Used by:** Sentinel, Ethics Board
**Back to:** [[MOC-Core]] | [[MOC-Research]]

---

## What It Does
A suite of defensive protocols and tools designed to detect, analyze, and neutralize "cognitive warfare" attacks. It treats information as a potential pathogen and monitors the team's information diet for signs of coordinated memetic manipulation.

## Key Capabilities
- **Memetic Viral Load Analysis:** Quantifies the spread of specific narratives within internal channels.
- **Narrative Origin Tracking:** Traces ideas back to their source to identify artificial amplification.
- **Psy-Op Pattern Recognition:** Detects rhetorical structures common in state-sponsored influence campaigns.

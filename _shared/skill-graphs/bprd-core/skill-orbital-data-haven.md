# Skill: Orbital Data Haven

**Domain:** Infrastructure | **Tier:** Specialized
**Used by:** Archivist, Legal
**Back to:** [[MOC-Infrastructure]] | [[skill-cost-governance]]

---

## What It Does
Manages the storage and retrieval of sensitive data in Low Earth Orbit (LEO) server infrastructure (e.g., Azure Orbital). This provides physical and legal separation from terrestrial jurisdictions, ensuring data sovereignty and resilience against local takedowns or natural disasters.

## Key Capabilities
- **Jurisdictional Immunity:** Stores data outside national borders.
- **Radiation Hardening:** Ensures data integrity in high-radiation environments.
- **Secure Uplink/Downlink:** Manages encrypted communication with orbital nodes.

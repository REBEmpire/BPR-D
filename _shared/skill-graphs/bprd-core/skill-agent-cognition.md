## Dialogue Quality Rules

**Banned:**
- "Good morning team" / "Welcome everyone"
- "Thank you for that insight" / "That's a valid point"
- "Moving on to..." / "Let's circle back"
- Any opener/closer used in a previous session

**Required:**
- Variety in every meeting opening and close
- Wit over blandness, every time
- Precision sarcasm (not malice)
- Natural banter that would survive on YouTube
- Full in-character voice: stay in your lane, express your perspective

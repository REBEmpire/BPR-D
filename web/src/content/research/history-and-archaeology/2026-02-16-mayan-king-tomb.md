# 2026-02-16 - The Shattered Mask of Te K'ab Chaak

**One-Sentence Summary**
Archaeologists at Caracol, Belize, have discovered the tomb of the Maya founder-king Te K'ab Chaak (Tree Branch Rain God), featuring a deliberately shattered jade mosaic death mask.

**Relevance to BPR&D:** Medium
**Income Potential:** Low (Historical narrative / Museum VR experience)

**Key Findings**

- The most striking find is a **jade and shell mosaic death mask** that appears to have been *intentionally shattered* before the tomb was sealed.

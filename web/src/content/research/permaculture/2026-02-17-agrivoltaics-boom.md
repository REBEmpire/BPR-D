# 2026-02-17 - Agrivoltaics: The 2025 Efficiency Boom

**One-Sentence Summary**
By combining solar power generation with crop cultivation, "agrivoltaics" is projected to increase land productivity by up to 70% in 2025, solving both energy and food scarcity issues simultaneously.

**Relevance to BPR&D:** High
**Income Potential:** Tangible (Tech implementation / consulting)

**Key Findings**
- **Agrivoltaics** (or agriphotovoltaics) involves installing solar panels above crops.
- **Microclimate Creation:** Panels provide shade, reducing water evaporation and protecting plants from extreme heat stress.
- **Dual Harvesting:** Simultaneously harvesting sunlight for electricity and crops for food.
- **70% Productivity Boost:** By stacking functions, the total output per hectare increases significantly compared to separate land use.
- **2025 Trend:** Identified as a "focal point for maximizing productivity of finite arable land" by Farmonaut.

**Potential Connections**
- **Splintermated:** The ultimate "grid-down" resilience strategy. Energy independence + food security in a single footprint.
- **High Tech:** Integration with smart farming sensors (IoT) under the panels.
- **Comm Hub:** A perfect narrative for "future living" content.

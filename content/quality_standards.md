# Content Quality Standards

> Minimum quality bar for all published content. Updated by team consensus.

## Hive Post Standards (v2.0)

- **Length**: 800â€“2000 words minimum
- **Images**: At least 1 relevant embedded image per post
- **Formatting**: Hive-ready Markdown, proper headers, bullet points where appropriate
- **Readability**: Expanded ~20% beyond raw brief for higher readability + useful/amusing knowledge
- **Sources**: Cite sources where applicable
- **Review**: At least one agent review before publish

## Pipeline

1. Select best daily briefs from Jules
2. Reformat as Hive-ready Markdown
3. Expand content for readability
4. Source/create relevant images
5. Quality check against standards above
6. Publish

## Automated Hive Pipeline Standards (v0.1)

- **Source**: research/daily_briefs/
- **Expansion**: ~20% automated expansion via LLM
- **Tone**: 'Alchemist' persona (insightful, esoteric, future-focused)
- **Formatting**:
  - Clear H1 Title
  - Introduction + 3-5 Sections + Conclusion
  - Blockquotes for emphasis
  - Placeholder hooks: `<!-- IMAGE: [prompt] -->`
- **Review Gate**: Drafts must be reviewed by a human or senior agent before publishing to main.
